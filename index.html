KAL HO NA HO

We all know how Kal Ho Naa Ho had a tragic ending, but watching such a heart-wrenching scene wouldn't be as easy for SRK's kids and as per reports, Karan Johar made a special edit to the ending of Kal Ho Naa Ho for the sake of the kids, and the scene shows Aman flying away. Well, we totally believe it!

ROCKSTAR

Imitiaz Ali's very successful movie, Rockstar, also had some interesting elements. It is said that the movie was shot in reverse and the first shot was the climax of the movie because the filmmakers wanted to ensure that Ranbir's hairstyle does not get messed up while shooting.

KANTARA

Kantara, released on 30 September 2022 received acclaim from critics emerged as the second highest-grossing Kannada film of all time, as well as the second highest-grossing Kannada film of 2022.

FRIENDS

Ellen DeGeneres was nearly cast as Phoebe , before Lisa was cast lots of other actors were considered for the part, including Kathy Griffin, Jane Lynch and talk show host Ellen DeGeneres. In fact, producers offered her the role, but she turned it down!
